## 4. CURRENT MENU ANALYSIS — JEFFERSON COUNTY (4-WEEK ROTATION)

### What's Working
- **Consistent structure** — Breakfast / Hot Lunch / Sandwich Dinner is clear and operationally simple
- **Yellow cake** is used heavily across all three meals — cost effective at $0.08–$0.11/serving
- **Cornbread** appears at every lunch and dinner — good value filler
- **Bean variety** is solid (pinto, northern, green beans, peas & carrots)

### Pain Points & Gaps
1. **Dinner is sandwich-only every night** — 4 proteins rotating (salami, turkey, PB&J, chicken patty). No hot dinner protein. This is nutritionally limited and monotonous for long-stay residents.
2. **Breakfast protein alternates only sausage and egg** — no variety across 4 weeks
3. **No fish** on the current menu — tuna/fish patty adds important nutrition and variety
4. **No vitamin C source** — no canned fruit, citrus, or fresh produce visible in the rotation
5. **Fortified beverage** is the only non-water drink aside from milk twice weekly
6. **Cake is the only dessert** — no gelatin, pudding, or fruit options which Shaver stocks cheaply
