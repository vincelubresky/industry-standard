Current Menu Assessment
4-week rotation analysis — what's working and where the gaps are
What's Working
Consistent Structure

Breakfast / Hot Lunch / Sandwich Dinner is operationally simple and clear

Yellow Cake Utilization

Used heavily across all three meals — cost effective at $0.08–$0.11/serving

Cornbread at Every Meal

Appears at every lunch and dinner — strong value filler with familiar appeal

Bean Variety

Good variety across pinto, northern, green beans, and peas & carrots

Pain Points & Gaps
1
No Hot Dinner Protein HIGH

Dinner is sandwich-only every night — 4 proteins rotating (salami, turkey, PB&J, chicken patty). Nutritionally limited and monotonous for long-stay residents.

2
Limited Breakfast Protein MED

Breakfast protein alternates only sausage and egg — no variety across the 4-week rotation.

4
No Vitamin C Source HIGH

No canned fruit, citrus, or fresh produce in the rotation. Shaver applesauce cups = $0.30/serving.

5
Dessert is Only Cake MED

No gelatin, pudding, or fruit. Shaver gelatin = ~$0.04/serving, pudding = ~$0.05/serving.

6
Limited Beverage Options LOW

Fortified drink mix is the only non-water option aside from milk twice weekly.
